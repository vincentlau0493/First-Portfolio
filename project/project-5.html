Introduction

Project Brief

RNexus(RN: registered nurses) is a collaborative social networking system that puts the patient back into the healthcare process securely based on iOS/ Android platform. This application would ideally be utilized for the patient to establish "care relationships" with their healthcare providers while receiving home health or hospice care services.

Function

  • integrating traditional face-to-face interaction with social networking technologies in order to establish a new kind of patient care experience
  • facilitating the enhancement of nurse-patient relationships by encouraging patients and their family members to communicate with their care providers
  • creating opportunities for patients to engage, learn, ask questions, and always be "in the know" of various aspects of their home care or hospice process
  • improving the quality of care by providing patients with an interactive communication platform in addition to a comprehensive care profile that is accessible securely any time or anywhere

Technologies

phonegap, interface design, prototype design, html, css, ajax, jquery mobile, php, mysql

Scenario

When patients leave the hospital but they still need continuous help from the professional workers, they will contact with nurse agencies like home health care and hospice agencies to assign some helpers like nurses and physicians. After calling to the agencies, the agency will assign a specific nurse to the patient. Everytime the assigned nurse helped the patient in home, they have to submit all the activities they have done and other updates of the patients medication profile.


To reduce the workload of the nurses and agencies, and to improve the communication for patients, an online health social-networking system is needed.

User Study

The majority of the user group can be divided into three different categories: Patients, Nurses and Agency managers.

Patients Features

  • Patients and designated family members have the option to sign up
  • Patients can track their assigned nurse
  • Patient will be able to instantly send medical records to another facility
  • Patient can confirm upcoming appointment
  • ......

Nurses(RN) Features

  • RN's can access current patient profiles once the patient has entered unique code
  • RN has access to a variety of templates to expedite the documentation process
  • RN's can document and make changes to the patient's care profile at any time
  • ......

Agency Features

  • Agency manager can stay in constant contact with RN's who are out working
  • Agency Managers are able to track RN's who are out working and record their logs
  • Agency Managers are able to monitor RN's activity by viewing posts and changes to a patient's profile
  • ......
